Privacy Policy

Effective July 23, 2026

Spillin Tea collects the name, email address, selected interest, signup source, and consent timestamp that you provide through our guest-list form.

We use this information to send opening announcements, event updates, soft-launch details, and related Spillin Tea news. We do not sell your personal information.

Security

We use reasonable technical safeguards, including restricted database permissions, server-side validation, and rate limiting. No online system can be guaranteed completely secure.

Contact

To request access, correction, or deletion of your information, contact Spillin Tea through the business contact information published on this website or our official social accounts.
